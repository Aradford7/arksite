KR Patreon PvPvE Custom Map Server Cluster

Our Public PvPvE casual cluster is oriented towards players who are looking to casually PvP and/or work through PvE content (caves, bosses, ascension, ect). These servers have significant rules in place to ensure clean gameplay. Offline raiding is exceptionally difficult, and will require larger tribes.   This cluster has PvP/Raid intentions similar to Offical PVP servers.

(Launch of this cluster is scheduled for indefinitely.   Map List still pending.

*If you are looking for more of laid back but still serious PVP with stricter rules, try out our Patreon PvPvE Cluster.   There are only stricter rules because it is a private patreon only server cluster, this cluster intended for the ones who have limited amount of gametime.

Custom Map PvPvE Cluster Settings and Rates

Connection from Steam Family Shared accounts are rejected by default.   You must post in Discord if you wish to be allowed to play on our servers if you are using a Family Share.

RATES
  • XP - 5x
  • Gathering - 4-6x
  • Taming - 10x
  • Maturation - 10x
  • Incubation - 10x
  • Imprint Interval - 10x
  • Cave Damage - 10x
NEW PLAYER PROTECTION
  • Automatically provides (7 days) of structure immunity.
  • Protection ends early when any tribe member reaches level 25
  • You can not damage structures while protected.
  • You may disable NPP early (tribe admins only)
  • Does not prevent PvP nor will it keep your dinos from being killed.
PLAYER SETTINGS
  • Max Player Level: 100 pre-Ascension, 115/130 Ascension 1/2, 135 max with Tek Cave
  • Player Speed Stats: .66x
  • Idle XP is heavily nerfed: .1x - Doing nothing (including just flying from Note to Note) gives very little XP reward. You will get FULL XP for Killing, Crafting and Harvesting.
DINO SETTINGS
  • Max Dino Levels: 150 (190 for Wyverns and Rock Drakes)
  • Titans (Extinction bosses) are tameable
  • 1 Titanosaur spawn per map
  • Aberration does not allow foreign dinos. See Wiki for Dinos that can transfer to Aberration
TRIBE SETTINGS
  • Max Tribe Size: 10
  • No Alliances: Setting disabled, but unofficial alliances are allowed for raiding, cooperation etc.
  • No alternative tribe members/names on different maps of our cluster
STRUCTURES
  • All S+ walls have 18k health
  • Structure resistance is .25
  • All turrets available at level 68
  • Turrets have a limit of 30 per 14 foundations. There is no limit to the max turrets that you can have.
  • A number of S+ structures/items are not in game for lag and PvP balance reasons.
