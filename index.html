Our Community

        We are over 1000 members strong, and many have been gaming together here for 10 years. We hosted a variety of different custom game servers from Gary's Mod, Conan Exiles, CS:GO, Dark and Light and ARK: Survival Evolved Servers!

        Our community is a family with members from varying lifestyles, beliefs and values: primarily adults with full time jobs and active family commitments or full-time students.

Drama Free Gaming

Our goal is to have fun and less drama. We care about the safety of our player base. KR is primarily a family friendly community that welcomes everyone, but we have a zero tolerance policy for anyone who violates any of our community standards.

Dedicated Servers

We host our own servers in our community to provide the best possible experience and connection to provide lag free gameplay and all day fun. We maintain our servers every 3am PDT to keep our servers always up to date and rubberband-free.

Private Patreon Servers

In addition to public severs, we offer private advanced PvPvE servers to our Patreons. These servers are for members only and have advanced rates and different set of rules. Just mature game play and good clean fun.

Strong Admin Presence

We have a number of admins from all timezones to help provide support nearly any time of day. They are very active in discord and in game.

Active Community

AGB has been gaming together for 18+ years. Our members have a very strong bond in game and out. We look out for one another!

Active Discord

Our Discord community is 2000+ members strong. Come hang out, get to know others and keep up on all things Ark. (Plus you can see other games AGB is involved with).

Public PvPvE Cluster

Our Public PvPvE cluster is open to all is geared towards more casual Players. These Servers have significant rules to ensure that new and casual players can have an opportunity to learn, socialize and progress, yet still have a life outside of Ark.
