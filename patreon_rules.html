PATREON SERVER RULES

The private Patreon PVP is an advanced accerelated PVP cluster. The rules below are setup to accomodate casual PvP. These rules are heavily enforced by our admin staff.

All tribes are encouraged to join our discord. Any issues and/or questions our staff has with any tribe will be resolved there. But if we can't find you in discord, we can't resolve any issues.

Violations of ANY rule listed on this website will have consequences depending on:

  • The rule violated.
  • Number of rule offenses.
  • Severity of violations (PvP/Raid or Non-PvP/Raid related).
  • History of yourself and/or tribemates.
  • Number of offenses.
  • Your's & your tribemate's attitude when Admins try to resolve the issue.
  • Joining our Discord is always a bonus in your favor.

Punishment/Consequences can include any combination of the following:

  • A warning.
  • A dinos or multiple dinos being executed.
  • Forfieture of ALL or partial Tribe inventory.
  • A player or all Tribe members being banned temporarily or permanantely.
  • A Tribe being banned from raiding temporarily.
  • Tribe wipe, to include any combination of: players, dinos or structures.
  • Compensations/repayments paid to a tribe you broke rules against during a raid.
  • Replacement of passive dinos killed execessivly during a raid or base spawn camping.
  • Removal of ORP & possible denial of ORP useage for the remainder or future of a seasons.

General Rules

  • ANY violation of the Community Code of Conduct
  • Tribe names must be the same on all servers in a cluster & players must belong to the same tribe across the cluster.
  • You are prohibited from changing Tribe and Dino names to hide your identity for PvP or raiding purposes.
  • You may not cage/incapaciate any player longer than 24 hours.
  • TRIBE INSIDING/SABOTAGE - Any player joining or leaving a tribe, using any methods or tactics that MAY be view as having the intent of sabotaging them for another tribe (aka 'insiding') will be permanently banned from our community.

Raiding / World PvP

  • Killing passive dinos in excess of what happens w/ splash damage will be viewed as toxic, & will lead to extra special Admin attention at the minimum.
  • Our community frowns upon complete wiping of a tribe's MAIN BASE. Don't do it.
  • Absolutely no foundation wiping, break what is neccessary to raid but DO NOT wipe an entire base to nothing.

Bases / Outposts

  • Tribes can have one Main Base (with ORP), and any number of additional or "auxiliary" bases or outposts on any map.
  • Bases and outpost must be 100% functional. Any structures found to just contain beds or a small storage containers will be removed by admins.
  • All tribes must remove ALL taming pens, spikes, traps etc after you are done using them if they are not at your main base (within the perimeter of it). Permanent Taming pens for global community are allow at certain location outposts talk to a GM or admin first for permission or we will remove it.
  • Any type of floating base or structure that takes no damage is prohibited. If an admin finds a structure built to 'float' after taking no damage, they will structure wipe your tribe to ensure no other floating structures exist.

Building Restrictions

  • Foundation / Pillar / Gate spamming is NOT allowed. You may not claim land that you cannot occupy with players and dinos immediately.
  • No blocking access (via buildings, turrets, dinos etc) to any obelisks, loot/artifact caves OR the passages into the Underworld on Center map.
  • Tribes are NOT permitted to block any new player spawns or have turrets near any spawn points.

Dino Rules

  • Luring wild dinos to other tribes bases is considered griefing.
  • Do not kill other tribe's passive tames if out in the open incase of owners returning back to retrieve their tame.
  • Do not kill a tribe's entire passive dinos during a raid.
